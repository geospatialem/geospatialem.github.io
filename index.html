About



Kitty is a GIS Developer and Cartographer located in Saint Paul, Minnesota with a background in analysis, cartography, enterprise GIS, emergency management and collaboration across multiple disciplines, departments and agencies. She is a certified GISP, Minnesota emergency management professional and SKYWARN storm spotter.When she's not making and breaking web apps she can be spotted hitting wrist shots on the ice as an assistant goalie coach with the Orono Spartans girls high school hockey team.
