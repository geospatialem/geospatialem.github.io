Kitty Hurley

About

Kitty is a front-end geospatial applications developer focused on UI/UX, cartographic design, mobile environments, and web accessibility. She has over eight years of progressive experience identifying and implementing innovative solutions, and coordinating and collaborating within the geospatial and development communities.

Kitty is an organizer of Maptime MSP and a Minnesota GIS/LIS Consortium conference volunteer. In her free time she likes to hike, snowshoe, play pond hockey, and travel.
