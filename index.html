About

A social geographer with a passion for collaboration, learning + traveling; Kitty is a GIS developer and cartographer with a background in geography, water resources and emergency management and collaboration across multiple disciplines, departments and agencies. She is a certified GISP, Emergency Manager and SKYWARN storm spotter. When she's not making and breaking web apps she can be spotted hiking, rollerblading, or ice skating around Minnesota.
